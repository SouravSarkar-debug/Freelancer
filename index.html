About Me

With over a decade of professional writing experience, I've developed a unique voice that blends emotional depth with commercial appeal. My background in theater and film production gives me a comprehensive understanding of what makes stories work across different mediums.

When I'm not writing, you can find me mentoring emerging writers through the Scriptwriters Foundation or hiking the trails near my home in Colorado.
